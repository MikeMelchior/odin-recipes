Tzatziki done the right way

Description


Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 1 medium English or hothouse cucumber,
    seeded, coarsely grated and squeezed as
    dry as possible (about 1 cup)
  • 2 small garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
    scant 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh mint
    (dill also works great in this recipe)

Steps:

  1. Grate your cucumber using a box grater or a food processor.

  2. Using a clean cloth, squeeze out as much liquid as you can from the grated cucumber

  3. Mix all of your ingredients together in a large mixing bowl, make sure to thoroughly incorporate everything together

  4. Serve with your choice of bread or veggies, also great as a spread for sandwiches and pitas!

  5. Try your best to save some for everyone else!
